A better way for schools to manage communication with parents

SkoolBase is one place for every school to manage communication with parents for newsletters, parent teacher interviews and notifications.

Interested to know more ?

(We are still in beta)

Smart schools communicate better

Not many, but one tool. SkoolBase is built to help schools be more efficient in managing all communication with parents from one single place.

Features

Parent Teacher Interviews

Schedule interviews in simple and easy steps. Parents can use the web, or their iOS or Android phones and devices to choose the teachers and times they want.

Newsletters

Build beautiful and content rich newsletters in minutes using our easy drag drop feature. Target the newslatters to groups or individuals.

Notifications

Keep everyone updated by sending instant push notifications to groups or individuals to their mobile apps or email.
